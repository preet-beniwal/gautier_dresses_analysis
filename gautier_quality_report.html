Strategic Recommendations & Conclusions

Evidence-based recommendations for quality enhancement and business optimization

1. Strategic Recommendations

A. Return Policy Optimization

Current State: "All sales are final" across all categories

Recommendation: Implement tiered return policy:

  • Store credit within 7 days for non-altered items
  • Maintain final sale for custom alterations and special orders
  • Exchange program for size-related issues within 48 hours

Expected Impact: 15-20% increase in customer confidence, reduced purchase hesitation

B. Digital Engagement Enhancement

  • Develop comprehensive customer review system on website
  • Enhance social media presence beyond Instagram (@gautierbridal)
  • Implement virtual try-on technology for online browsing
  • Create style guide content featuring real customer photos

C. Pricing Transparency Improvements
